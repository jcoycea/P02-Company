Text Input Field with Label


CSS selector:

.text-label
input[type="text"]

Sample code:

<label for="input-name" class="text-label">Email Address</label>
<input type="text" id="input-name">

</div>

Branding

Branding Ideas

What does the company do, or offer?

Olderbrother makes universal garments, equally tailored to fit men, women and people.

Who is the company to potential customers?

Olderbrother is a playful, eco-sustainable, naturally dyed, gender-neutral contemporary collection from Portland for the slow fashion revolution.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

They construct their whimsical unisex clothes from sustainably harvested fabrics like organic Japanese cotton and use vivid dyes made from almost exclusively edible pigments. They draw inspiration from ingredients that are animating the wellness world at that particular moment—fiery-orange turmeric, golden saffron, the muted earth tones of healing mushrooms.

Fonts

Europa is the brand typeface.

Used for headings and body text.

"Europa is a geometric sans-serif typeface designed by Swiss designer Fabian Leuenberger and released through EuropaType in 2011. The design is unusual in that it has humanist influences in addition to the geometric influences—Leuenberger claims the two main influences for Europa were Futura and Gill Sans." – Typewolf

Muli is the supporting font.

Used for navigation and user interface elements.

"Muli is a free, open-source sans-serif typeface designed by the prolific Vernon Adams, creator of numerous other open-source fonts available on Google Fonts." – Typewolf
